The Super Spleen Adventure!

What's This Funny-Looking Organ?

The spleen is like your body's special superhero HQ! It's about the size of your fist and lives under your rib cage on the left side.

Fun Fact!

The spleen can change size - it gets bigger when it's working hard!

It might look like a big purple bean, but it's one of the most important parts of your immune system!

The Spleen's Superpowers!

Your spleen has three amazing jobs that help keep you healthy:

Blood Filter

Acts like a sieve to remove old or damaged red blood cells

Germ Fighter

Helps your immune system battle germs and infections

Blood Storage

Keeps extra blood ready in case you need it fast

Did You Know?

The spleen holds about a cup of blood at any time - that's enough to fill a juice box!

How the Spleen Helps Your Tummy

While the spleen isn't part of the digestive system like your stomach or intestines, it plays an important support role:

  • 1. It watches for bad germs that might come from food
  • 2. Helps recycle iron from old blood cells, which your body uses to make new blood
  • 3. Works with the liver to clean your blood

Fun Fact: When you eat, your spleen actually gets a little bigger as it helps process nutrients from your food!
